BALI

No island fascinates tourists like Bali. Even though it is less than the size of a large island in Hawaii, Bali manages to bring a variety of adventures. Stunning beaches feature beachgoers and surfers along the island's southwest coast, while ancient temples and a thriving community wellness draw tourists to
Ubud.

LABUAN BAJO

Labuan Bajo and Komodo National Park are an inseparable entity. The two are interconnected so that when you are in Labuan Bajo, you need to visit the Komodo National Park. To get there, you can use a ferry or fast boat. There are many departure options from morning to evening.

RAJA AMPAT

The Raja Ampat Islands are in the westernmost part of Papua and stretch over an area of approximately 4.6 million hectares. Raja Ampat Regency consists of 4 large islands namely Waigeo, Batanta, Salawati and Misool Islands, and 1,847 other small islands.

BOROBUDUR

Located in Magelang Regency, Central Java, this magnificent and beautiful temple has been recognized by local and foreign tourists as the largest Buddhist temple in the world. Naturally, because Borobudur Temple has an area of about 2500 square meters, with a length of 121.66 meters, a width of 121.38 meters and a height of 35.40 meters.
