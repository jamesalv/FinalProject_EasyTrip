What you will experience

Explore the Nusa Penida island, a developing island destination southeast of Bali known for its beautiful natural surroundings. Bring your pals or go beach hopping with other tourists in Nusa Penida for the day. You will see some of Penida's most well-known attractions, such as Kelingking Beach, which has a headland in the shape of a T-Rex and is a must-see for everyone. Then get ready for a short hike from a cliff's edge to the stunning Atuh Beach and the adjoining Diamond Beach, which is the newest beach destination on the island. Take plenty of shots while you enjoy the feel of the sea wind on your face and the stunning views of the azure waves to receive your much-needed dose of sea!.
